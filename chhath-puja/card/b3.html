More About the Event

Chhath Puja is dedicated to the Sun God, Surya, and his wife Usha, thanking them for bestowing life on earth and seeking their blessings. The festival is celebrated with great devotion and enthusiasm, especially in Bihar, Jharkhand, and Uttar Pradesh.
