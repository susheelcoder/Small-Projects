About VoiceByKajal

I am Kajal, a singer from Sitamarhi, Bihar. Music has always been a very important part of my life, and I love expressing emotions through my voice. I sing Hindi and Maithili songs along with devotional and cultural music that reflects the traditions of Bihar.

Through platforms like YouTube and Instagram, I share my songs and my musical journey with people who enjoy meaningful and regional music. Singing allows me to connect with listeners and share feelings that words alone cannot express.

VoiceByKajal is my small effort to share my voice with the world and build a community of listeners who appreciate music, culture, and authentic storytelling through songs.

Why Voice By Kajal

The name Voice By Kajal represents a unique identity for sharing voice content online. It clearly shows that the voice, ideas, and stories shared on this platform come directly from Kajal.

This name is simple, memorable, and easy to find across social media platforms like YouTube, Instagram, and Facebook.

Using the same name everywhere helps build a strong digital presence so people can easily recognize and connect with the voice behind the content.
